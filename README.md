## Cau Chuyen | The Story

Minh la **Le Van An**, mot ky thuat vien laptop tai Viet Nam.

Hang ngay minh mua ban, sua chua hang tram chiec laptop cu. Va minh nhan ra mot van de nhuc nhoi: **may bi fake cau hinh tran lan**. RAM 4GB duoc sua registry thanh 16GB. CPU i3 bi doi ten thanh i7. O cung 128GB hien 512GB. Nguoi mua khong co cach nao biet -- cho den khi qua muon.

Minh tim kiem phan mem kiem tra, nhung:
- **CPU-Z, HWiNFO** chi doc tu mot nguon, de bi danh lua
- **Phan mem co phi** qua dat cho ky thuat vien nho le
- **Khong co phan mem nao** kiem tra tat ca trong mot: pin, man hinh, ban phim, USB, loa, camera...

Nen minh tu viet. Tu mot script PowerShell nho chay tren USB, qua 3 nam phat trien, **LaptopTester** da tro thanh cong cu duoc hang nghin ky thuat vien tin dung.

> *"Toi tung mua phai may fake RAM. Tu khi co LaptopTester, chua bao gio bi lua lai."*

## Tai Sao Khac Biet? | Why Different?

### Chong Fake Da Nguon
Khong chi doc tu Windows/WMI -- LaptopTester so sanh cheo **Registry, SMBIOS, va BIOS** cung luc. Neu co thong so nao bi sua, phat hien ngay lap tuc.

<p align="center">
  <img src="screenshots/antifake.png" alt="Anti-Fake Detection" width="700">

### Test Pin Thuc Te
Khong chi bao "pin 96%". LaptopTester cho ban biet **suc khoe that**, chu ky sac, dung luong goc vs hien tai, va chay **stress test pin** bang workload thuc te (trinh duyet, Office).
